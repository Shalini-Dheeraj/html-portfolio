Shalini's Recipe for the best chicken sanwich

Ingredients

  • 4 pieces of bread
  • 250 grams of Chicken
  • 1 onion
  • Half table spoon of pepper
  • Half table spoon of oregano
  • Table spoon of tomato sauce
  • Table spoon of Green chilli sauce
  • Table spoon of Butter

Instructions

  1. Add a spoon of butter and fry onions
  2. Once it turns golden colour, add salt, pepper, oregano, tomato sauce and green chilli sauce to it and mix all.
  3. Mince the boneless meat
  4. Add minsed meat into the pan
  5. Saute until the chicken is cooked
  6. Stuff the filling inside the bread with cheese added as optional
  7. Toast it
